About

My Story

I am a UX designer with a background in psychology. I have experience in an applied role as a civilian contractor with the army, where I designed programs to cultivate well being in wounded soldiers. Empathy, creativity, and collaboration led to programatic success. To date these programs continue to support soldiers in many forms of recovery. I would like to take what I have learned, working with a unique population, and apply these lessons in a role as a UX designer and strategist where impact can be designed to help other populations in need. I would love the opportunity to collaborate, or simply toss some ideas around with you and your team. Be sure to check out my portfolio and connect with me on LinkedIn.
